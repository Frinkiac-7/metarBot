metarBot

Plan, track, and store your flight weather data

metarBot is a weather utility for operators of small unmanned aerial systems. It's designed to help reduce the need to check multiple websites for weather reports, meteorological aerodrome reports (aka METARs), and other sources used by commercial and hobbyist drone pilots.

Get Airport Information & Details

You can use metarBot to get airport information such as location, codes, GPS coordinates and runway details.

Station METARs and TAFs

Access Meteorological Aerodrome Reports (METAR) and Terminal Aerodrome Forecasts easily with metarBot .

NOTAMs directly from the FAA

metarBot gets you up to date Notice to Airmen (NOTAMs) directly from the Federal Aviation Administration.

Planned Features

metarBot's future includes dashboards for easy access to frequently used data, general weather forecasts, and much more.
