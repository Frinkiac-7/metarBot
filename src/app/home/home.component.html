metarBot

Plans, tracks, and stores your flight weather data

metarBot is a weather utility for operators of small unmanned aerial systems. It's designed to help reduce the need to check multiple websites for weather reports, meteorological aerodrome reports (aka METARs), and other sources used by commercial and hobbyist drone pilots.

No account required!

Use metarBot without having to sign up. Just click the button below access the app dashboard.

One account with everything!

By creating an account you get access to all of metarBots features like dashboard customizations and adding favorites.
